Sony Aero-V1

Flight, reimagined.

Structural Dynamics

Ultralight Carbon Lattice

Precision-molded carbon fiber wings and composite landing gears offer extreme structural rigidity at a mere takeoff weight of 249g.

This enables up to 45 minutes of stable hovering flight, defying extreme wind conditions up to Level 6.

Sensory Grid

Spherical LiDAR Array

Equipped with 360-degree LiDAR and dual forward binocular cameras to map surrounding obstacles in real-time.

  • Active 3D spatial mapping protects your investment.
  • Navigates dense forests and tight indoor paths seamlessly.
Optics & Capture

1-inch Exmor T Sensor

Features a high-dynamic range 1-inch sensor capable of 8K HDR filming at 60 frames per second.

With stacked pixel structures and low-noise processing, captures cinematic shots during sunset or high-contrast shadows.
