Processing Core

The SONY V3 Neural Audio Engine

At the center of the computational audio loop sits our proprietary SONY V3 chip. Built on a ultra-low-power 4nm architecture, it houses double the neural compute cores of previous chips.

It processes ambient noise arrays and applies anti-noise filters 12,000 times per second, adapting dynamically to atmospheric pressure and head movement in real-time.

Atmospheric ANC Algorithms

By using interactive multi-point feedback, our V3 algorithm isolates and target specific frequency spectrums — from high-frequency commuter voices to deep airplane cabin hums.

Audio Quality

40mm Bio-Cellulose Dome Drivers

The high-compliance diaphragm is engineered from lightweight, rigid bio-cellulose fibers. Supported by a high-flux magnetic coil, it responds instantly to micro-transients.

This prevents speaker membrane flexing, preserving absolute fidelity and eliminating harmonic distortion even during extreme bass sweeps or transient orchestra swells.
